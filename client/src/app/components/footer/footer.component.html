FAQ

What is zero-knowledge proof technology?

Zero-knowledge proof (ZKP) technology allows one party to prove to another that they know a value without revealing the value itself, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

How does your anti-AI phishing authentication system work?

Our system uses ZKP to verify user identities without revealing sensitive information, making it highly resistant to AI-driven phishing attacks.

How can your technology reduce server load and increase security?

ZKP minimizes data processing and transfer during operations, reducing server load and increasing security by keeping sensitive information hidden.

What advantages do your decentralized applications offer over traditional blockchain solutions?

Our decentralized applications are faster and more efficient than traditional blockchain solutions, with enhanced privacy and security through ZKP.

What products do you offer for embedded system security and monitoring?

We provide real-time monitoring and threat detection solutions for embedded systems, ideal for IoT, automotive, and mobile devices.
